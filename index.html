TwitchToolkit

The complete list of commands are below with descriptions

  • !bal - check balance and karma rate
  • !buy - Syntax: !buy beer 6 - purchase an item or event
  • !whatiskarma - explains what the karma system is
  • !buylist - gives users a link to the public purchase list and puppeteer link
  • !lookup - lookup an item that may not have shown up on the buy list
  • !price - displays the price in chat for an item
  • !instructions - gives users info on using the mod
  • !research - gives users info about the current research being undertaken
  • !modsettings - gives user info on how many coins are being earned and global settings
  • !giftcoins - Syntax: !giftcoins @username 100 - give another viewer your coins
  • !toolkitcmds - shows all available commands to the user, this does not translate by default so it can be universally recognized.
  • !join - join the queue to be assigned to colony
  • !buy pawn - Syntax: !buy pawn human - purchases a pawn to join (see races below)
  • !story - view information about your pawns backstory
  • !skills - view information about your pawns skills
  • !stats - view pawn's statistics
  • !health - view pawn's health
  • !needs - view pawn's needs
  • !>gear - view pawn's gear
  • !body - view pawn's health conditions
  • !work - view pawn's work priorities
  • !story - view pawn's background and traits
  • !pup - pulls up website for puppeteer
  • !leave - viewer leaves colony
